Events

IOSD Promises exciting events for rejuvenation and fun-filled training.


Startup Showcase

As amazing as you may be, there's always someone better than you. The wise people learn from others’ mistakes. Bring your startups to show them off and watch those of others’ to learn and ponder “Why didn't I think of this?”

Global Innovation Festival

All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. IOSD members of all chapters come together to let their hair down and have the best time of their lives at the Global Innovation Fest every year.

Amateur Entrepreneurship Challenge

For eleventh and twelfth class students, IOSD provides a problem-solving platform with Amateur Enterpreneurship Challenge where the students will suggest and implement app-based solutions to technical problems.

IOSD Students Chapter Fest

Tech-addicts never get enough of technology. Catering to their heart's desires, IOSD conducts fests in every single chapter! Before you run off, grab your dancing shoes too.

IOSD Gaming Festival

Gamers cheer up for here's the piece of heaven for you. IOSD conducts the Gaming Festival with various gaming events and fab prizes.

IOSD Annual Meetup

Teaching newbies, learning from pros, taking responsibility, feeling proud when people deem an IOSD event a phenomenal success, all of it is part and parcel of the journey for us.

Synergy'19

The successor of last year’s most famous Tech Fest of Delhi at Delhi Technological University, Synergy 2.0 came in the lives of the young techies and started an adventurous journey. This 24 hours Hackathon hosted by IOSD DTU team witnessed great talents from colleges across the country.

Internship Fair

On 30th March this year Students from all over Delhi gathered at Netaji Subhash University of Technology. It was North India’s biggest internship fair organised by a dedicated team of IOSD, more than 1000 students from 60 different colleges, 25+ emerging startups looking for talents and companies like JIO, The Times Network and many more came to the fair. More than 90% of the participants got their dream internship.

HackGTBIT

IOSD started its spring hackathon season with a bang with a collaboration with more than 9 top colleges in Delhi. We invited students from all around Delhi to participate in this tech event. IOSD’s HackGTBIT being one of them. A 12 hour Hackathon was organized at the premises of Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology where we witnessed great tech talent brainstorming and building mind-blowing projects.

HackBPIT

A first-ever 24 hours Hackathon of BPIT was organized by the Team of IOSD BPIT. We had a great line of sponsors who helped us to make this hackathon bigger than we ever imagined with a participation of more than 500 students accommodated at the college itself. We made sure that they receive the best hospitality and a great environment to code and build overnight. A big Shout out for the organizing Team. Their efforts paid off.

HackMSIT

The HackMSIT was held on 8-9th March 2019. The Event entrenched a humble beginning of a new technological era with the engagement of students from different colleges in and around Delhi. IOSD MSIT team worked hard day and night to host the best Hackathon of the season. More than 30,000 lines of code where everyone had one mission- to create a revolutionary product. With a beautifully decorated premise and best hospitality offered by the team HackMSIT lifted the benchmark for upcoming hackathons.

Impulse’19

Nothing is impossible for IOSD and we proved it. The second edition of the most renowned technical fest of IP university “Impulse’19” broke all the records that were ever made. A 36 hours Tech fest had many events including a 12 hour Hackathon , and PubG Battle fight and many more kept the interest and engagement of the young techies intact, with prizes worth 1 lakh. All the participants had an incredible time and went back to their home with a big smile. IOSD MAIT’s team made it possible.

Hack JU

HACKJU is the first-ever hackathon at Jadavpur University , Kolkata on 2nd March 2019. Group of techies explored their creativity having one goal in mind - 'Technology complementing current healthcare infrastructure' and they pushed it to another level.
